The Promotion Journey in ITP

Please treat this place like a future workplace

2021/09/20

Modo met the new teacher, Mr. Yeung, but his rude attitude and behavior towards the students left a bad impression on him.

2021/10/04

Modo officially submitted her application for the IT Prefect position to Ms. Lai. After waiting for three years, she finally became an ITP.

2021/11/29

On Modo's first day of duty, although she had to start from the bottom, Mr. Yeung reminded her to treat it as if she were working in a professional environment. She was already aware that any difficulties would eventually be overcome.


During this time, due to the pandemic, the school implemented a half-day schedule with attendance limits for morning assemblies, allowing only one or two forms to enter the Hall D at a time. As a result, her work was quite light and easy. As a trainee, Modo spent the entire semester mainly performing patrolling duties.

2021/12/10

Modo's Inner Monologue
Back then... primary 5 years was the darkest time of my life. That exchange program changed my life forever.

2022/2/17

In the next semester, Modo, who is sensitive to interpersonal relationships, noticed that ITP was no longer overseen by her computer teacher, Ms. Lai, but had been taken over by Mr. Yeung. Modo began to worry about her promotion prospects, knowing that her new supervisor lacked ethics and responsibility.

2022/06/10

As Modo's last duty as a trainee, the pandemic limited her opportunities to perform, leaving her with little chance to showcase her abilities. With her supervisor's reassignment, Modo started to worry about her chances for promotion next year.

2022/10/05

Modo felt anxious about the delay in announcing the new Form Head list. Meanwhile, an alumnus of the school, Arthur Leung (Hin), returned to FLSS as an IT technician and joined the ITP Team.

2022/10/21

During the student council Q&A session, the first major event for ITP this year, Modo was unexpectedly assigned to on-site duty due to Mr. Yeung’s lack of oversight. She collaborated with Hin for the first time and met Ms. Kwong, who was in charge of Hall A. The event concluded successfully, but unfortunately, many attendees were dissatisfied with their performance.


Since the beginning of 2022, Mr. Yeung’s behavior had caused dissatisfaction among many teachers and students. Over time, they began to associate the entire ITP team with his shortcomings, leading to misplaced anger directed towards them. Modo felt frustrated because if this continued, the ITP department might be disbanded. Having come to see this place as her second home, she did not want to lose her sanctuary. To change the situation, Modo decided to report everything to Ms. Lai, including matters regarding the Form Head list that has not yet been announced.


For the first time, Modo experienced the challenges of having a demotivating supervisor. Knowing that being the whistleblower might not yield positive results, she still took this step for the team and began to understand how the workplace operates.

2022/10/24

At this time, there were already two factions forming: the old faction led by Ms. Lai, including Modo, Hin, and others studying ICT under Ms. Lai; and the new faction led by Mr. Yeung, composed of ITP members he personally invited to join.


Modo deeply felt the culture of cliques in the workplace.

2022/12/19

After learning about everything that had transpired over the year, Ms. Lai decided to hold a review meeting—the first of its kind for ITP in many years. The meeting primarily addressed the issues of order in the control room and behavior, subtly indicating to Mr. Yeung the responsibilities he should uphold. She announced that the new personnel appointments would be revealed on 2023/01/30.


Modo felt the benefits of having a good relationship with her senior manager, but her rapport with Ms. Lai was genuine and not simply for personal gain.

2023/01/30

Modo was officially promoted to IT Form Head Prefect.

2023/01-04

In the months following her promotion, due to ongoing mismanagement, Ms. Lai assigned Sunny, the head of the IT department, to oversee the control room operations. Sunny often chatted with Modo, mainly about Mr. Yeung, and invited her to visit the server room, which she found friendly and approachable.

2023/05/05

Modo's largest event in her ITP career was entrusted to her by Ms. Lai, assigning her the responsibility of managing the sound in the auditorium. Despite feeling familiar with the equipment after a year of training, she still felt nervous during the event. Fortunately, thanks to the synergy with Hin and Ms. Lai's guidance, the event proceeded smoothly. Modo received positive feedback from many teachers about ITP, successfully restoring some of its reputation.


For the first time, Modo experienced the joy of seeing her hard work pay off.

2023/05/27

Like the award ceremony, the sound control for this graduation ceremony will be entirely managed by Modo, allowing her to gain significant experience and aiding her promotion path. Additionally, it garnered positive feedback for ITP, particularly from Ms. Tang, who had previously been critical of the ITP team. She even praised Modo's outstanding performance to Ms. Lai, increasing Modo's promotion chances.


However, Modo remained aware that ITP was no longer under Ms. Lai's leadership, understanding that her promotion opportunities ultimately depended on Mr. Yeung.


This was the first time she felt the overwhelming and daunting nature of workplace power dynamics.

2023/07/12

Mr. Yeung required all IT Prefect to fill out an annual survey, ostensibly to understand the work situation this year. However, Modo saw through the true purpose of the survey: he wanted to identify who was dissatisfied with him and whom he could recruit into his faction.


Since this questionnaire is not anonymous, Modo completed it half-heartedly. She successfully and richly navigated through her year in ITP.

2023/09/14

This day was originally scheduled for the promotion ceremony, but due to Mr. Yeung's negligence, it was nearly canceled. Modo felt very anxious because she had not yet confirmed her promotion to IT School Head Prefect. After Modo strongly advocated to Ms. Lai, she successfully postponed both matters by a month.

2023/09/20

Modo began shooting promotional videos with Hin and her peers.

2023/10/03

Modo was officially promoted to IT School Head Prefect, and the principal personally presented her with the work badge during the morning assembly.

2023/11/06

A new female colleague, Vivian, joined the IT department. Upon meeting her, Modo thought that Vivian was very timid, and they all felt she needed some time to adjust.

2023/12/13

Since Modo had a good relationship with Sunny, she often went to the server room to chat with him. However, Modo began to notice a subtle atmosphere, especially in Sunny's attitude towards Vivian. For a moment, Modo thought they had become a couple, and she felt very awkward about this assumption.

2024/01/05

After the weekly assembly, Modo could no longer hold back and decided to ask Hin about the situation. Hin sighed, relieved that someone had finally noticed, and revealed to Modo the secret he had discovered:

Sunny was already married but was having an affair with Vivian, even taking leave together to travel. Although this did not concern the ITP team, Modo felt very disappointed in Sunny. Hin even advised Modo to stay away from him and exposed the fact that Sunny was two-faced. He appeared enthusiastic in inviting students into the server room, but when discovered, he would shift the blame onto others.


At this moment, Modo decided to write a novel after graduation to commemorate the events of the past three years, titled "ITP Survival Diary".


Modo felt completely disillusioned with this workplace. She once believed that there could be genuine relationships in the workplace, but in reality, you could be stabbed in the back by someone and still thank them. At the same time, she felt angry about having to become two-faced herself.

2024/01/12

After class, Modo ran into Hin, who told her that he had informed Ms. Lai about everything. He also pointed out that there was an unauthorized camera in the control room directly connected to Sunny's phone. Ms. Lai was aware of it but lacked the ability to resolve the issue. At that moment, Modo had a bold idea: as School Head, she had the responsibility to protect the privacy of every IT Prefect. She decided to find an opportunity to cut the power to the camera and shared this plan with Hin.


Modo had always believed that this action was inappropriate, but when she found herself in a situation where even her supervisor could not help the team, she decided to step up and take action. As the saying goes, with great power comes great responsibility.

2024/01/16

After the morning assembly, Modo and Hin were the only ones left in the control room. Modo quickly covered the camera with paper and disconnected the power. She had a backup plan: if the camera had memory, they would know who the "culprit" was. Modo would explain that the situation was too chaotic, and she accidentally unplugged the power cord.


In retrospect: Modo realized that she was no different from others in the workplace, all capable of playing “games”. Yet, she believed her actions were for the good of the team. On one hand, she felt anger towards her own methods; on the other, she felt proud to contribute to the team.


After the action, Hin informed Modo that Sunny had personally brought someone in last year to lighten the IT department's workload. However, that person upset Sunny, and he began to bully him at work, ordering him to replace the network cables in every classroom in the school. In the end, he even framed him to Ms. Lai, leading to his dismissal.


Modo felt the harshness of the workplace and the ugliness of human nature once again.

2024/02/21

Modo had been diligently preparing for the DSE, and her ITP work was progressing smoothly. It was only after the event that she found a moment to chat with Hin about the recent developments. Hin revealed that one day, while entering the server room as usual, he had witnessed Sunny and Vivian engaged in intimate acts. That same day, he reported the incident to the principal, who subsequently reprimanded both of them. Upon hearing this, Modo felt a wave of discomfort wash over her. This meant that Sunny was now aware that Hin had uncovered his secret, and since Modo was part of the same team as Hin, she risked being implicated as well. Yet, despite her unease, Modo felt a sense of resolve; she had anticipated that this day might come.


Hin mentioned that workplace bullying had been happening for a while; Modo just hadn’t had the chance to talk to him about it. Fortunately, after working diligently at the school for a year, Hin had built up a considerable network. So, when Sunny planned to resort to his old tricks to frame him, Ms. Lai could help protect Hin’s position amidst the rumors.


Modo used to believe that it wasn’t necessary to establish connections with many people in the workplace, but through this experience, she realized the power of networking.

2024/03/08

For Modo, this was the last day of her secondary school classes and also the final day of her ITP journey. After bidding an emotional farewell to the mixer panel that she had worked with for three years, she began to focus entirely on preparing for the DSE and getting ready to embark on a new chapter in her life. The ITP journey had come to a end, but her personal life chapter was just beginning.


Although in the end, neither Modo nor Hin achieved success, and the wrongdoers did not receive their just deserts—Sunny remained in his position due to his power within the school—Modo had personally experienced the harshness of the workplace and the ugliness of human nature over these three years. She learned how to navigate the professional world, which had become a valuable lesson. During her time in ITP, she also discovered her future path. Given her strong interest in the mixer panel, Hin encouraged her to pursue a goal in Creative Media at City University of Hong Kong.

In the future

Modo's story in ITP has long come to an end, but beyond her journey toward promotion, there are many moments and anecdotes from her time in ITP that I wish to share with you, as well as with the junior members still in the ITP team. I hope this novel can truly serve as a survival manual.

Mr. Yeung

Mr.Yeung (M)

Position:

Second-in-Charge of the ITP Team

Introduction

A teacher joined FLSS when Modo in Form 4 and immediately stirred up a lot of controversy. Eager to rise quickly, he actively took over the ITP team. However, due to his irresponsibility and poor attitude towards others, he made many teachers and students dislike him within just two months, severely affecting the order and reputation of the ITP team.


After Modo graduating, with no one monitoring his behavior, the ITP team effectively became defunct. He then transferred to "harm" Campus TV, as the ITP team held no value for him anymore.


Even now, despite receiving a large volume of complaints, he still works at the school.

He enjoys workplace bullying, which makes him seem kind, but he is actually very deceitful.

Modo Lau

Introduction

In form six year, Modo was finally promoted to School Head IT Prefect. Just before graduation, she discovered Sunny's shocking secret and helped Hin and Ms. Lai lead the team out of the shadows. One of her most notable achievements was personally dismantling the hidden camera that Sunny had installed in the ITP workspace and reporting him to the principal.


After going through various experiences, she begins to understand society better, so her expression look heavy and helpless. Her outfit is based on my school uniform. A slight smile only appears when chatting with Hin.

My Memory - 2017/04/01

On the third day of the program, we went to the supermarket with our teachers to buy food. Naturally, I wanted to buy some snacks. At dinner time, when the teacher found out I had bought some treats, she suddenly threatened me, saying, "If I find out you bought unnecessary things, I’ll take them all away and only return them on the day we go back to Hong Kong." I felt she wasn’t joking, but the problem was that other classmates had bought snacks too—why was I the only one being scolded? That night, unexpectedly, the three teachers in charge of the exchange program burst into my room and took away another classmate, forcing me to face their questioning alone. They demanded I unpack my luggage for inspection and even coerced me into calling my family to falsely accuse me. I felt utterly helpless and could only apologize to them, feeling very wronged. When I mentioned a souvenir I bought for my cousin, they immediately laughed at his name, believing I was lying.

My Memory - 2017/04/03

However, on the last day of the program, we went to the local supermarket one last time to buy souvenirs. I was just short of money to buy a box of snacks, and since there was no mention of not allowing borrowing money at the project preparation meeting , I borrowed from my best friend. She agreed, but as soon as those three teachers saw me talking to someone else, they rushed over, grabbed my arm tightly, and forced me to look at her while answering her questions. I was terrified and stammered my responses, ultimately saying one wrong thing. That was enough for her to accuse me of lying. She even whispered in my ear, "If you lie again, I’ll tell all the teachers and students at the school about your deceit." At that moment, over 40 classmates were curious about what was happening. In the end, I could only wrongly “admit” to lying, which seemed to satisfy them. When it came time to pay, as I took out my money, they loudly exclaimed, “Looks like someone doesn’t have enough money!”

My Memory - Back to HK

Back at school, it was they who broke their promises. A teacher who had previously been familiar with me suddenly changed her attitude 180 degrees. The position of discipline captain, which was originally supposed to be mine, was suddenly given to someone else. Clearly, they had informed all the teachers in the staff room about me.

Since then, I have yearned for power. I suppose I wanted to make up for the regrets of my primary school days. So when I learned about the IT Prefect position in the first year of secondary school, I wanted to apply. However, due to grade restrictions, I had to wait until form 4 to officially pursue the dream I had in primary 5.
